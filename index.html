Recall AI – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Recall AI ("the App") is developed by Black Mirror Lab. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

Recall AI does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All recordings, transcripts, and notes are stored locally on your device. We have no access to your data.

2. Microphone Access

The App requires microphone access solely to record audio on your device. Audio is processed locally using Apple's on-device speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer). No audio is sent to external servers.

3. Speech Recognition

The App uses Apple's on-device speech recognition framework. Apple's privacy policy governs any data processed by Apple's frameworks. We do not receive or store any speech data.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Storage

All recordings, transcripts, and PDF exports are stored exclusively on your device. You are responsible for backing up your data. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
